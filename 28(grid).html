The main difference is in how much space they take up. display: grid acts like a big table, covering the whole width. display: inline-grid is like a small table that fits its content. grid-column-start:1: grid-column-end:4: grid-row-start:2: grid-row-end:4: means the item will start from row 2 and end at row 4 read css-tricks
